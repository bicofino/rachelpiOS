Area of a Square

Lesson Objective
In this lesson, we will learn about the area of a square.
About This Lesson
In this lesson, we will:
  1. Learn about the formula for the area of a square.
  2. See an example on using the formula to find a square's area
  3. See another example on using the formula to find the length of the sides (edges).
The study tips and math video below will explain more.

Study Tips

Tip #1
A square has four right angles and all the sides of a square have the same length.

Now, if the length of each side is L, the area, A of the square will be:
A = L2

The math video below will give more explanations on this. Also, we will see some examples on how to use the formula.
