Question 1
Which of the following terms have only one variable?

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A. I and II
B. III and IV
C. III and V
D. None of the above
Check Answer

Step by Step Solutions 

Step 1
For the first term, a and b are variables. Therefore, this term has 2 variables
Step 2
For the third term, z and p are variables. Therefore, this term has 2 variables
Step 3
For the second term, x is a variable. Therefore, this term has 1 variable
Step 4
For the fourth term, t is a variable. Therefore, this term has 1 variable.
Step 5
For the fifth term, p and q are variables. Therefore, this term has 2 variables
Step 6
The third and fourth terms (III and IV) have only 1 variable each. Clearly, the answer is B.
