Graphs: Coordinates of a Point

Lesson Objective
Learn how to read & write the coordinates of a point.
Why Learn This?
To use the coordinate plane, the first thing you must learn is to read & write the position (coordinates) of a point on the plane.

The picture on the right helps to illustrate further.

Now, let's go through the study tips listed below.

Study Tips

If you are not sure about x-coordinate or y-coordinate, just keep the tips in mind first. You will be clear about it while watching the math video.
Tips #1
Read the x-coordinate first, then the y-coordinate. This minimizes mistakes when you write down the coordinates.
Tips #2
Double check the coordinates that you have written down. Make sure the x-coordinate and y-coordinate are in the correct order and not swapped.
