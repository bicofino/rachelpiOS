Question 2
Given the Coordinate Plane as shown in the picture, name the two parts marked with '?'.

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A. y-axis and Quadrant 2
B. x-axis and Quadrant 4
C. y-axis and Quadrant 4
D. x-axis and Quadrant 3
Check Answer

Step by Step Solutions

Step 1
One of the part marked '?' is the y-axis. This axis is also known as vertical axis.
Step 2
The other part is Quadrant 4.

Note that the Coordinate Plane has 4 quadrants and the sequence of each quadrant is in
anti-clockwise order 
Step 3
From Step 1 & 2, clearly the answer for this question is C.
