Question 1
Simplify the following expression.

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A. 3x + 4
B. 7x + 2
C. 3x + 2
D. -3x + 2
Check Answer

Step by Step Solutions 

Step 1
First, arrange the like terms as shown.
Step 2
Now add the terms +3 and -1 together. You will get +2.
Step 3
Now add the like terms +5x and -2x together. You will get 3x.
Step 4
There is no more like terms in this expression. This expression cannot be simplified further.
Step 5
The final expression is 3x +2. Therefore, the answer is C.
