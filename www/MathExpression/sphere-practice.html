Question 1
Given a sphere with the radius 7cm. Find the volume of this sphere. Take π as 3.14.

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A. 1436.03 cm2
B. 1436.03 cm3
C. 87.92 cm2
D. 87.92 cm3

To calculate the volume, we can use the formula for the volume of a sphere. Note that r is the radius of the sphere.

Step 4

Step 5
Clearly, the answer is B.
