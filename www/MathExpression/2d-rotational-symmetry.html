Question 1
Which of the following figures have 2D rotational symmetry with their order of symmetry correctly labeled?
I

Order 1
II

Order 2
III

Order 6
IV

Order 5

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A. I and II
B. II and III
C. III and IV
D. I and IV
Check Answer

Step by Step Solution 

Step 1
When we rotate this shape for 360o, we will realize that it only fits onto itself once.

Hence, this shape has order of symmetry of 1 but has no rotational symmetry.
Step 2
When we rotate this shape for 360o, we will see that it fits onto itself twice.

Therefore, this shape has rotational symmetry and rotational symmetry of order 2.
Step 3
When we rotate this shape for 360o, we will see that fits onto itself 6 times.

Therefore, this shape has rotational symmetry and rotational symmetry of order 6.
Step 4
The flower's petals are different in shapes. Therefore, it can only fits onto itself after one full turn.

Hence, this shape has symmetry of order 1 but has no rotational symmetry. 
Step 5
From the steps above, only figures II and III has rotational symmetry with correctly labeled order of symmetry. Hence, the answer is B.
