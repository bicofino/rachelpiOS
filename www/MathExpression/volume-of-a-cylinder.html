The study tips and math video below will explain more.

Study Tips

Tip #1
If we have a cylinder with the radius r and height h, the volume, V of the cylinder is:
V = πr2h
where π is a number that is approximately equals to 3.14.

The math video below will give more explanations about this formula. Also, we will see some examples on how to use it.
