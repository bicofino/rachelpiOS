Combining Like Terms Practice Question

Question 2
Which of the following pairs are like terms:

Answer

Select and check your answer...
A.  All of the above
B.  I, III and IV
C.  I, II and IV
D.  I, II and III
Check Answer

Step by Step Solutions 

Step 1
The terms in I are like terms. This is because both terms have the same variable x2
Step 2
The terms in II are like terms. This is because these terms have the same variable x2 and y3 even though both variables are written in a different order.

Step 3
The terms in III are unlike terms. This is because these terms have variables with different exponent (i.e. power).
Step 4
The terms in IV are like terms. This is because both terms have the same variable m
Step 5
The terms in I, II and IV are likes terms. Clearly, the answer is C.
